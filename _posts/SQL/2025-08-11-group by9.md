## 테이블 구조

1. `FISH_INFO`테이블
    - `ID`: 잡은 물고기의 ID
    - `FISH_TYPE`: 물고기의 종류(숫자)
    - `LENGTH`: 잡은 물고기의 길이(cm)
    - `TIME`: 물고기를 잡은 날짜

## 답안


```sql
SELECT COUNT(*) AS FISH_COUNT, MAX(IFNULL(LENGTH, 10)) AS MAX_LENGTH, FISH_TYPE
FROM FISH_INFO
GROUP BY FISH_TYPE
HAVING AVG(IFNULL(LENGTH, 10)) >= 33
ORDER BY FISH_TYPE
```
